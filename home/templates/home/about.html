About

Welcome to GT Movie Hub, your go-to platform for movie recommendations from fellow Georgia Tech students! We've created a community-driven space where Yellow Jackets can share and discover great films, making it easier to find your next movie night pick between study sessions.

Our platform lets you browse recommendations across all genres. Each review comes from a real GT student, giving you real perspectives from our diverse campus community.

Join your fellow students in building Tech's own movie recommendation database. Whether you're looking for a study break film or planning a movie night with your dorm, GT Movie Hub helps you find the perfect watch based on real student experiences.
